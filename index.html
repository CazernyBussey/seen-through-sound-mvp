Speak life into someone’s day.

Your voice could be the reason someone keeps going today. Record a short message of hope, strength, or encouragement for someone who needs a reminder that they matter.

Seen Through Sound is a voice-powered encouragement platform created by Even Though I’m Blind, Inc. Submitted messages are reviewed before publication, and only approved messages may be shared publicly as part of the encouragement library.
